Biography

Siddharth Gosavi is currently a Consultant in Internal Medicine at Sai Gosavi Speciality Clinic,Nano Hospitals Uttarahalli,Nano Hospitals Hulimavu and Saraswati Speciality Clinic Nagarbhavi.Siddharth Gosavi was a Senior Resident in the Department of Internal Medicine at Kasturba Medical College Manipal.He has completed his MD General Medicine/Internal Medicine at the Department of Internal Medicine, JJM Medical College Davanagere and is now an Internal Medicine Physician.He has completed his DNB Internal Medicine,a degree awarded by the National Board Of Examinations New Delhi. Siddharth does research in Internal Medicine (General Medicine).He has published extensively in Pubmed,Scopus and Directory of Open Access Journals. He is an Academic Editor for PLOS One Journals. He also is a freelance peer reviewer for BMC Public Health,Plos One,Frontiers and Cureus Journal Of Medical Science.

He also is an accomplished singer and has multiple studio recordings.He is also a Member of the American College of Physicians,an Academic Life Member of Research Society For The Study Of Diabetes In India,A Life Member of the Association Of Physicians Of India and Karnataka Chapter,an Academic Life Member of Innovative Physicians Forum,A Life Member of the Indian Society Of Hypertension,A Life Member of The Indian Association Of Clinical Medicine,Annual Member of Clinical Infectious Diseases Society of India,Life Member of the Indian Society of Medical Mycologists and a Fellow Of Academy Of General Education.He is a recipient of the ACP Early Career Physician Award 2022,ACP Scientist Of The Year Award 2023 and the Dr M Vishwanathan Poster Award 2020.

Medical Education

Dr. Siddharth Gosavi holds a distinguished medical background, completing his MBBS from Vydehi Institute Of Medical Sciences and Research Centre in 2016. He furthered his expertise with an MD in General Medicine from JJM Medical College, Davangere in 2021, and subsequently obtained a DNB in Internal Medicine from the National Board Of Examinations (NBE), New Delhi in 2022. Prior to his medical education, he excelled academically, scoring 91% in his 12th STD CBSE from Sri Sathya Sai Higher Secondary School (2010) and 91% in his CBSE from Delhi Public School, Bangalore South (2008).
