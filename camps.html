7th July 2024

A Free Medical Camp was organised successfully on 7th July 2024 Sunday at our clinic. The camp was received well and patients made use of glycemic profile, lipid profile and hypertension screening. Emphasis on cardiovascular health was put forward too in the camp. August 11th 2024

A Free Medical Camp was organised at Sai Gosavi Speciality Clinic on August 11th Sunday between 4pm and 8pm. The camp consisted of Physician Consultation, Hba1C measurement, Lipid Profile measurement, Random Blood Sugar measurement and Hypertension screening. Dermatology consultation was provided by Dr Chinmayee Anand and ENT consultation was provided by Dr Yoshitha Natesh.The importance of Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus, Hypertension and Dyslipidemia were elucidated to all the patients.

25th August 2024

A Free Medical Camp was organised at Sai Gosavi Speciality Clinic on 25th August 2024 between 4 pm to 8 pm. The camp consisted of Physician Consultation, Serum Uric Acid detection and Vitamin B12 deficiency awareness. The importance of looking out for symptoms of high uric acid, foods to be avoided in patients with high uric acid were stressed upon.Patients were also made aware about how Vitamin B12 deficiency affects overall health and how early symptoms must be reported and treatment started early.
